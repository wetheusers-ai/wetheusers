# The Constitution (and Bill of Rights)

The governing framework: distributed authority and checks, sortition, amendment
rules, and — the hardest article — speech and moderation. Treated, with the source
code, as the operational constitution of the network ("code is law").

Historical analog and warning: the U.S. Articles of Confederation (1781) were the
first attempt at governance and **failed**, and had to be rebuilt as the
Constitution (1787). Expect a "second founding."

**Status:** later phase, after launch. See `../founding-brief.md` §4.4–4.5 for the
hardest open problems.
