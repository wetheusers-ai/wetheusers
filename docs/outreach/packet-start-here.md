6. **"Ambition Against Ambition"** — a short essay on how a user-owned internet governs itself, and checks its own power.
7. **"The Longer Race"** — a short essay on the economics of building together: at least as much value, more evenly shared.

You don't need all of it to have the conversation; the first three are enough.
